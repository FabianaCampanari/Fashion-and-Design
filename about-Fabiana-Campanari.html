about

Welcome to my creative space, my name is Fabiana Campanari. I was born in São Paulo – Brazil. After graduating in psychology at the São Paulo Pontifical Catholic University (PUC /SP) I moved to California in 1998 to study at the UCSD University where I acquired a degree in graphic design.

Upon returning to Brazil in 2002 I had the chance to enhance my skills working on São Paulo’s leader communication agencies such as Power4, Index Design and Trust Design. Having the opportunity to develop full package design projects for Brazil’s top brands, ranging from telecommunication companies to industry, supermarkets to retail stores and from sports to culture events.

In 2007 I initiated a solo career specializing in the creation and production of innovative and exclusive fabric pattern designs for selective fashion brands. Please check my fashion design portfolio.
